WEB DEVELOPER

I am a well rounded developer with a good sense of design that loves to dive into projects and provide creative and effective Front End solutions. Whenever I get my hands on a project, I never simply "just work" on them, I treat it as if it was my own product; I put passion, care and a personal touch to every product I work on. I am proficient with HTML5, CSS / LESS / SASS, JavaScript, JQuery, and have solid understanding of Ruby on Rails and PHP. As a fast learner, I am always open to picking up new and upcoming languages. Have any suggestions? Get in touch!
